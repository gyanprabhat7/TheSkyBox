About

    The SkyBox is an arcade-style game in which the player controls the Box Character,
    have the goal of jumping through boxes persistently. The player is tasked with navigating the Box
    through randomly generated moving sky boxes that have gaps placed at random places. Box
    Jumps when the player taps the touchscreen.
    Each successful jump throug the boxes awards the player one point.
    Falling in the void ends the gameplay.
